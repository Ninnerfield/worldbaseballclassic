The World Baseball Classic(WBC) is an event like the FIFA World Cup, where players from all over the world represent their country and compete in a tournament to decide which country is the best at baseball. This event happens every four years and it is just starting as I write this. However, it seems most people I talk to about the WBC are not aware of what it is. So I want to create a website to be informative about what it is.
